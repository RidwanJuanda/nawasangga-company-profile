VPA-2x2 USB

The VPA-2x2 USB is an audio interface (often referred to as a sound card) manufactured by Victory Pro Audio. It is designed for home studio recording, podcasting, and live streaming, offering professional-grade resolution in a portable desktop format.

VPA-4x4 USB

A professional audio interface (external sound card) from the Victory Pro Audio line. While the 2x2 series only has two inputs, the 4x4 series is designed for more complex needs, such as recording live bands, podcasts with multiple speakers, or more complete home studio setups.

Victory Pro Audio's wireless microphone system utilizes UHF (Ultra High Frequency) frequencies. This series is specifically designed for professional applications such as music stages, houses of worship, meeting rooms, and even high-end karaoke.

VPA-AM6

A compact 6-channel audio mixer from Victory Pro Audio. Designed as a versatile solution, this mixer combines traditional analog features with modern digital technology (USB/Bluetooth), making it ideal for home studios, podcasting, or small-scale music events.

VPA-PM6

A 6-channel Powered Mixer from Victory Pro Audio. Unlike the AM6 series, which is a passive mixer, the PM series (Powered Mixer) is equipped with an internal amplifier, so you can directly connect it to passive speakers without the need for an additional power amplifier.
